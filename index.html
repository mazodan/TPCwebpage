Tuguegarao Tricycle Price Checker app

Make Tricycle operators accountable using crowdfunding to identify overcharging drivers

Description: The Tricycle Price Checker app developed using Thunkable is an app that collects fare data from passengers to identify how much it costs to go to another barangay. The barangays that are listed for now are from Tuguegarao City. The origin, destination, number of passengers and fare is uploaded to a database (Firestore) and when it is queried, the app calculates the minimum, maximum and the median of the fare to find out how much it actually costs to go to the destination.
